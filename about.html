About Dr. Sohail Farooq Magoo

Leading Pain Management Specialist with Over 25+ Years of Experience

Welcome to Advanced Pain Management

Dr. Sohail Farooq Magoo is a highly experienced Consultant Pain Physician with over four decades of expertise in interventional pain management, regenerative medicine, and anesthesiology.

Since beginning his medical career in 1983, Dr. Magoo has been dedicated to providing advanced and compassionate pain care, with a special focus on musculoskeletal, spinal, and regenerative therapies.

With a passion for innovation and patient care, Dr. Magoo has treated thousands of patients across Pakistan, pioneering new techniques and providing relief to those suffering from chronic pain conditions.
