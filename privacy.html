Privacy Policy

How we protect your personal and medical information

Last Updated: October 2025

Dr. Sohail Farooq Magoo (MBBS,PGD) and our clinic are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or use our services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information that you provide to us when booking appointments, including:

  • Personal identifiers (name, address, phone number, email)
  • Medical history and health information
  • Insurance information
  • Payment details
  • Communication preferences

This information is essential for providing you with medical care and managing your treatment.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Provide medical treatment and care
  • Schedule and manage appointments
  • Communicate about your treatment and follow-up care
  • Process payments and insurance claims
  • Comply with legal and regulatory requirements
  • Improve our services and patient experience
  • Send appointment reminders and health information (with your consent)

3. Data Protection and Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal and medical information, including:

  • Secure, encrypted data storage
  • Limited access to authorized personnel only
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Secure communication channels
  • Compliance with healthcare data protection standards

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. Medical information may be shared with:

  • Healthcare providers involved in your care
  • Insurance companies for claims processing
  • Legal authorities when required by law
  • Laboratory or imaging facilities for diagnostic purposes

All sharing is done in compliance with applicable healthcare privacy laws and only with your explicit consent or as required by law.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal and medical information
  • Request corrections to inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information (subject to legal requirements)
  • Obtain a copy of your medical records
  • Opt-out of non-essential communications
  • Lodge a complaint with relevant authorities if you believe your privacy rights have been violated

6. Medical Records Retention

We retain medical records in accordance with legal requirements and professional standards. Generally, records are kept for a minimum of 7 years from the date of last treatment, or longer if required by law.

7. Website and Cookies

Our website may use cookies and similar technologies to improve user experience. These may collect:

  • Browser type and version
  • Pages visited and time spent
  • Referring website
  • IP address (anonymized)

You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences.

8. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services for appointment scheduling, payment processing, or communication. These providers are required to maintain confidentiality and security of your information.

9. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children without parental consent. If treating a minor, we require parental/guardian consent and involvement.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the new policy on our website with an updated revision date.
