Prolotherapy - Our Signature Treatment

What is Prolotherapy?

Prolotherapy, also known as proliferative therapy, is a regenerative injection treatment that stimulates the body's natural healing process to strengthen and repair injured and painful joints, ligaments, and tendons.

How It Works

  1. Assessment: Thorough examination to identify damaged or weakened tissues
  2. Injection: Natural solution injected into affected ligaments and tendons
  3. Healing Response: Body responds by increasing blood flow and stimulating repair
  4. Strengthening: New collagen fibers form, strengthening the treated area
  5. Recovery: Gradual reduction in pain and improvement in function over weeks

Benefits of Prolotherapy

  • Non-surgical, minimally invasive procedure
  • Treats the root cause, not just symptoms
  • Promotes natural tissue regeneration
  • Minimal downtime and recovery
  • Long-lasting results
  • Alternative to surgery and long-term medication

Who Can Benefit?

Prolotherapy is ideal for patients experiencing chronic pain from ligament or tendon injuries, arthritis, sports injuries, and degenerative conditions who wish to avoid surgery.

Patient Education & FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

What should I expect during my first visit?

Your first visit includes a comprehensive evaluation of your medical history, physical examination, review of any imaging studies, and discussion of your symptoms. We'll develop a personalized treatment plan tailored to your specific condition and goals.

Is prolotherapy painful?

Most patients experience minimal discomfort during the procedure. Local anesthetic is used to numb the treatment area, and any soreness typically resolves within a few days.

How many treatments will I need?

The number of treatments varies depending on the severity and location of your condition. Most patients require 3-6 sessions spaced 3-6 weeks apart for optimal results.

Is prolotherapy safe?

Yes, prolotherapy has an excellent safety record. The solutions used are natural substances that stimulate healing without significant side effects. Serious complications are extremely rare.

How effective is prolotherapy?

Clinical studies show prolotherapy to be highly effective, with success rates of 75-90% for many conditions. Results are typically long-lasting when combined with appropriate rehabilitation.

Will insurance cover my treatment?

Coverage varies by insurance provider and plan. We recommend contacting your insurance company directly. Our office can provide documentation to support your claim.

What is the recovery time?

Most patients can return to normal activities within a few days. Strenuous activities should be avoided for 1-2 weeks after treatment to allow for optimal healing.
