Prolotherapy is an injection-based treatment designed to stimulate the body's natural healing at weakened ligaments and tendons. Treatments are tailored to each patient and may include targeted injections, rehabilitation, and follow-up care.

Benefits

  • Reduces chronic joint pain
  • Improves stability and function
  • Non-surgical alternative

Process

  1. Assessment and diagnosis
  2. Targeted injection(s)
  3. Rehabilitation and follow-up

Who is it suitable for?

Patients with ligament or tendon laxity, chronic joint pain, or those seeking non-surgical options may be candidates. A full assessment is required.
