Frequently Asked Questions

What should I bring to my first appointment?

Please bring any relevant medical records, imaging results (X-rays, MRIs), list of current medications, and your insurance information if applicable.

Do you accept insurance?

We work with most major insurance providers. Please contact our office to verify your specific coverage.

How long is the first consultation?

Initial consultations typically last 45-60 minutes to ensure we have time for a thorough evaluation and discussion of treatment options.

Can I get same-day appointments?

While we try to accommodate urgent cases, same-day appointments are subject to availability. Please call our office for the earliest available slot.

What is your cancellation policy?

Please provide at least 24 hours notice for cancellations. Late cancellations or no-shows may be subject to a fee.

Do you offer virtual consultations?

Yes, we offer telemedicine consultations for follow-up appointments and initial assessments. Ask about this option when booking.
